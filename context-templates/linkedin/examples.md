# LinkedIn Examples

> Gold standard reference posts. The setup wizard will replace these with YOUR examples once you provide writing samples.

[PLACEHOLDER — After running /content-setup, this file will contain 3-5 of your best posts as reference examples for tone, structure, and quality matching.]

## What Makes a Good Example

Each example post should demonstrate:
- Your natural voice (humor, directness, storytelling style)
- The hook-twist-pitch structure working naturally
- Correct length (400-850 chars)
- A specific point of view, not generic advice
- Something only YOU could write — your experience, your proof, your angle

## How Examples Are Used

When the content pipeline creates a LinkedIn post, it reads these examples as the quality bar. The frameworks describe the structure; these examples show the execution.
