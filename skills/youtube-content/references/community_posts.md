# YouTube Community Posts — Best Practices

> Reference for the cadence command. Based on platform research as of March 2026.
> YouTube renamed "Community" tab to "Posts" in early 2025. Functionally identical.

---

## Post Types (Ranked by Engagement)

| Type | Engagement | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Polls** | Highest (7,700+ votes vs ~1,000 likes on regular posts) | Topic selection, opinion gauging, prediction questions |
| **Image polls** | High | Visual paired with voting options |
| **GIFs** | Medium-high | Reactions, humor, meme culture around news |
| **Image posts** | Medium | Infographics, data viz, benchmark comparisons, screenshots |
| **Text posts** | Low-medium | Announcements, discussion prompts, questions |
| **Embedded video** | Lowest | Avoid as primary format — text/image post with link performs better |

**Key insight**: Polls consistently outperform everything else by 5-7x. A text/image post with a link to a video outperforms an embedded video post.

---

## Posting Frequency

- **Sweet spot**: 2-3 posts per week
- **Hard ceiling**: No more than 1-2 per day (triggers algorithmic suppression)
- **Minimum**: 1 per week to maintain activity signal
- Quality > volume at smaller subscriber counts

---

## Image Specs

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| Max resolution | 1600 x 1600px |
| Recommended minimum | 1080 x 1080px (square, 1:1) |
| Landscape | 1920 x 1080px (16:9) |
| Aspect ratio range | 2:5 to 5:2 |
| Formats | JPG, PNG |
| Images per post | Up to 10 (carousel) |
| GIF max size | 16 MB |

**Display note**: YouTube shows a square crop preview. Full image reveals on click. Design for the square crop.

---

## Text/Character Limits

| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Post body | ~7,700 chars |
| **Visible before "Read more"** | **288 chars** (treat as hook) |
| Poll text options | 65 chars each, up to 4 options |
| Poll image captions | 36 chars each |
| Quiz answers | 80 chars each |
| Quiz explanation | 500 chars |

**Formatting**: No markdown support. No bold, italic, headers, or bullets. Only line breaks work. Paragraph breaks are the only structural tool.

---

## Where Posts Appear

1. Channel's Posts tab (always visible)
2. Subscribers' Subscriptions feed (app)
3. Home feed (algorithmically surfaced to subscribers)
4. Home feed (non-subscribers — unpredictable but real discovery)

---

## Algorithm Effects

- Posts generate engagement signals that boost channel-level recommendation priority
- Pre-video polls warm up the audience — voters are primed to click the video
- YouTube evaluates channels across ALL formats (video, Shorts, Posts) for quality signals
- Inactive Posts tab is a mild negative signal
- No direct ranking boost to individual videos, but indirect through audience warm-up and channel activity

## Tactical Playbook

### Pre-Video Teaser (24-48h before upload)
Post a poll: "Which should I cover: X or Y?" or "Prediction: will X actually work?"
Then reference the poll results in the video's first 10 seconds.

### Breaking News Reaction
Screenshot of announcement + your commentary in the post text.
Low effort, high relevance, taps into timely interest.

### Insight Carousel (Image Post)
1080x1080 square images with key takeaways from a recent video.
Dark background + bright accent colors (matches tech aesthetic).
Up to 10 images per post.

### Discussion Prompt
"Hot take: [contrarian position]. Am I wrong?" — drives comments.

### Behind-the-Scenes
Screenshot of your actual workflow, terminal, dashboard.
"Here's what my setup looks like at 2am."

```
Option B: [65 chars max]
Option C: [65 chars max]
Option D: [65 chars max]
```

### Image Post Template
```
[Hook line — under 288 chars, this is what shows before "Read more"]

[CTA: question or call to action]
```

### Text Discussion Template
```
[Provocative statement or question — under 288 chars]
```
