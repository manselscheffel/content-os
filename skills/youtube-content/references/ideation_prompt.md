# Video Idea Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric to score and rank video ideas from the 5 intelligence sources. Each idea is scored 0-10 across 4 dimensions.

## Scoring Dimensions

### Timeliness (0-3 points)

How urgent is this topic? Will it be stale if you wait?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 3 | Breaking — happened in last 48 hours. Competitor just posted, tool just launched, major announcement. First-mover advantage is real. |
| 2 | Trending — rising search volume, multiple signals in the last week. Window is open but not closing immediately. |
| 1 | Evergreen with a hook — the topic is always relevant but there's a timely angle (new update, seasonal relevance, recent controversy). |
| 0 | No urgency — could make this anytime. Fine for backlog but not this week. |

### Audience Fit (0-3 points)

Does this match your ICP? Which archetypes does it serve?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 3 | Hits 3+ archetypes. Directly addresses a top pain point from icp.md. Your audience is searching for this. |
| 2 | Hits 2 archetypes. Relevant to their work but not their #1 pain point. |
| 1 | Hits 1 archetype. Niche but valuable for a specific segment. |
| 0 | Doesn't clearly map to any archetype. Interesting but wrong audience. |

### Competitive Advantage (0-3 points)

Can you make this video better than anyone else?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 3 | You have direct experience/proof nobody else has. You built it, tested it, broke it. Your system IS the proof. |
| 2 | You have a contrarian angle with genuine merit. You know something the competitor missed or oversimplified. |
| 1 | You can add depth but don't have unique proof. Your take is better-researched but not fundamentally different. |
| 0 | Commodity take. Any creator could make this same video. No differentiation. |

### Pillar Coverage (0-1 point)

Does this fill a gap in your content distribution?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | This content pillar (from competitive-strategy.md Tier system) is underrepresented in your recent output. Making this balances your channel. |
| 0 | This pillar is already well-covered recently. |

## Total Score

Add all dimensions: max 10 points.

- **8-10**: Make this video this week. High urgency, high fit, strong angle.
- **6-7**: Strong candidate. Queue it for next available slot.
- **4-5**: Decent idea. Keep in backlog, may combine with another angle.
- **1-3**: Pass. Doesn't justify the production effort right now.

## Source Weighting

When scoring, consider the reliability of the source:

- **Competitor angles** are pre-validated — someone already proved audience interest. Bonus if their video got high views but low satisfaction (comments show frustration).
- **News items** are time-sensitive — score 8+ from the news monitor means your audience is already talking about this. But news decays fast.
- **SEO trends** are search-demand validated — people are actively looking for this. Best for evergreen + timely hybrid topics.
- **ICP gaps** are strategic — nobody's covering this, which means less competition but also unproven demand. Higher risk, higher differentiation.
- **Novel ideas** are your moat — original builds and frameworks can't be copied. But they need to be framed in terms of viewer benefit, not just "look what I built."

## Output Format

Present ideas as a ranked table, then expand each with detail:

```
| Rank | Idea | Source | Score | Timeliness | Fit | Advantage | Pillar |
|------|------|--------|-------|------------|-----|-----------|--------|
| 1 | ... | competitor_angle | 9 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 0 |
| 2 | ... | seo_trend | 8 | 2 | 3 | 2 | 1 |
| ... |
```
